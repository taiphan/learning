## Weekly time split (10h) with video

```
10 h/week
├── 4–5 h  Lab (non-negotiable — app Lab tab)
├── 2–3 h  Video / reading (Coursera, LinkedIn, YouTube — this doc)
├── 1–2 h  BRD / banking context / Track B templates
└── 0–2 h  Track B on milestone weeks only (8, 16, 28, 40, 52)
```

**Anti-pattern:** 8h Coursera + 0h lab → you will fail CP0 and interviews.  
**Pattern:** Watch one module → pause → reproduce in `lab/exercises/` same day.

## Verify before you commit

1. Open course → **Enroll** → look for **“Full Course, No Certificate”** (free) vs **Preview** (module 1 only).
2. Class Central course page shows enrollment type before you start.
3. For LinkedIn: confirm trial end date in account settings.
4. Add completed external courses to CV only after you can demo matching **`lab/` project**.
