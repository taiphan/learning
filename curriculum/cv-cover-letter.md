## 4. Tips

- Lead with **business outcomes**, then the technology that delivered them.
- Quantify everything: %, currency, volume, time-to-value.
- Show **production scale** and **leadership**, not just modeling.
- Demonstrate **governance/responsible AI** as built-in, not bolted-on.
- Mirror the JD's language (Predictive AI, Generative AI, MLOps/LLMOps, ROI).
