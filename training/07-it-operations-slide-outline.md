## Slide 2 — Learning objectives
- Control delivery without doing business work
- Triage catalog SRs — IT support only, delegate business work
- Run BOD/EOD rituals, roster, weekday vs weekend coverage
- Handle incidents — business owns cases and allocation

## Slide 3 — Scope boundary (IT vs Business)
- IT owns systems; Business owns case/rules/allocation/data decisions
